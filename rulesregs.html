Tribe Alliances

The Soul Eaters tribe is dedicated to camaraderie, strategy and diplomacy. We are a fair tribe that views success as something measured through the development of our tribesmen. If we attack, the attack is supported by those capable. If we are to defend, we rally in defense. Expect nothing less than what you perceive.

We are aware, we are awake. Do not be part of those the Soul Eaters take!
